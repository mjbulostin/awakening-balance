Thai Bodywork & Massage Therapy

Awakening Balance : Thai Bodywork

Brought to you with ❤ by Christina Law

Thai Bodywork is an ancient, healing type of bodywork. The recipient wears comfortable clothing and lies down or is seated on a soft, thick massage mat. The practitioner guides the person through a series of postures while massaging the muscles and applying pressure along the body's energy lines and pressure points.

After almost 2 decades of practice and being trained originally in Thailand, Christina’s sessions are unique where she also incorporates Vedic Marma Therapy and Pranic Healing for a full holistic approach.
