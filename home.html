About Christina

I began my journey in France and started dancing at the age of 4 years old. At 16, my family and I moved to the US and I discovered Yoga and the health benefits of detox after going through some health challenges. In the year 2000, I received my 200RYT and taught Yoga full time in Atlanta for many years. I went to Thailand a few years later to receive my certification in Thai Bodywork and went through more deep detoxification experiences. I trained with Sarah Powers in Yin Yoga and received my 100RYT in Yin and meditation.

In 2008, I completed my 500RYT. After an intense car accident and much pain, I found the Faster EFT method and Ayurveda that helped me regain control of my health and life. I co-owned Yoga Wellness of Blue Ridge studio for a few years and taught Yoga Teacher trainings. I now manage a beautiful retreat center called Heartwood Health and Yoga in Blue Ridge.

I am here to help others with all of these tools I have integrated within myself over the last 2 decades and on the way to becoming an Ayurvedic Practitioner and Ayurveda Yoga Therapist. I see clients via Zoom for Yoga, emotional release (hypnotherapy, Faster EFT) ,health and life coaching, Ayurvedic consultations. I also do Thai Bodywork at Heartwood Health and Yoga in our lovely studio, offer workshops and events. I am the organizer for Dancing Tree Festival, a wonderful Yoga and sacred music festival that happens in our neighborhood every other year.
